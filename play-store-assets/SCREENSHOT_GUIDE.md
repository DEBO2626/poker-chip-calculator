# Play Store Screenshot Guide

## Required Screenshots

Google Play requires **at least 2 screenshots**, but 4-8 is recommended.

### How to Take Screenshots on Galaxy S25

Press **Power Button + Volume Down** simultaneously

---

## Recommended Screenshots to Take

### Screenshot 1: Mode Selection (Home Screen)
**What to show:**
- The main screen with "Auto-Calculate Tournament" and "Custom Stack Amount" buttons
- Shows users the two main modes

**Steps:**
1. Open the app
2. Make sure you're on the home screen with both mode buttons visible
3. Take screenshot (Power + Volume Down)

---

### Screenshot 2: Entry Tier Paywall
**What to show:**
- The Entry Tier paywall that appears when clicking Auto-Calculate
- Shows the $0.99 pricing clearly

**Steps:**
1. Clear app data first (Settings → Apps → Poker Chip Calculator → Storage → Clear Data)
2. Open app
3. Click "Auto-Calculate Tournament"
4. Entry Tier paywall appears
5. Take screenshot

---

### Screenshot 3: Auto-Calculate Results
**What to show:**
- A completed calculation showing chip distribution
- Demonstrates the core functionality

**Steps:**
1. Make sure you have Entry Tier activated
2. Click "Auto-Calculate Tournament"
3. Fill in sample data:
   - Players: 8
   - Duration: 4 hours
   - Inventory: Use defaults
4. Click Calculate
5. Take screenshot of results

---

### Screenshot 4: Premium Paywall
**What to show:**
- Premium upgrade screen
- Shows Custom Stack feature and pricing

**Steps:**
1. Make sure Entry Tier is activated (but not Premium)
2. Click "Custom Stack Amount" from home
3. Premium paywall appears
4. Take screenshot

---

### Screenshot 5 (Optional): Custom Stack Setup
**What to show:**
- Custom Stack input form (if you have Premium activated)
- Shows advanced features

---

### Screenshot 6 (Optional): Chipset Management
**What to show:**
- Saved chipsets screen (Premium feature)
- Shows user can save configurations

## After Taking Screenshots

1. **Transfer to PC:**
   - Connect phone via USB, or
   - Upload to Google Drive, or
   - Email to yourself

2. **Save to folder:**
   - Save all screenshots to: `C:\Users\john_\Desktop\Poker chip\play-store-assets\screenshots\`

3. **File names:**
   - `01-mode-selection.png`
   - `02-entry-paywall.png`
   - `03-auto-calculate-results.png`
   - `04-premium-paywall.png`
   - etc.

## Screenshot Requirements

- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Dimensions:**
  - Minimum: 320px
  - Maximum: 3840px
  - Aspect ratio: 16:9 recommended
- **File size:** Max 8MB each
- **Number:** 2-8 screenshots

Your Galaxy S25 screenshots will automatically be the correct size!
